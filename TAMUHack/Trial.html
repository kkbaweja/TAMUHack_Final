SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP LEARNING LAB

The Social Entrepreneurship Learning Lab (SELL) Fellowship Program is an intensive six-part fellowship program for undergraduate students attending the University of Texas at Austin who want to build social impact-focused organizations. Learning Lab sessions include lectures, discussions, workshops, and pitches intended to equip passionate students with a social entrepreneur’s essential toolkit.
